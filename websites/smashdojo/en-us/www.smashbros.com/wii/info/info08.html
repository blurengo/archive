Target Smash!! and Home-Run Contest World Records

  

Through Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection, a vast number of Target Smash!! and Home-Run Contest submissions have poured in from players of all regions.

I distribute one submission per day, but it's not really obvious which submissions are setting records, is it?

To that end, I put together a list of the world records for each character for you to take a look at. The data is current as of the end of July, 2008.

"What?! How did someone pull THAT off?" ...I'm sure most of the records will surprise you into saying stuff like that. They're simply amazing...

On the other hand, if you've already broken these records...please post your record using Nintendo WFC!

Once I get a new batch of submissions, I'll update the list. So, keep sending them in!
